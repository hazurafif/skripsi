User Manual

Welcome to the Hand Gesture Recognition Application!

In this application, you can use your hand gestures to communicate with your computer or device in a variety of ways. Whether you want to control your media player, make a presentation, or just have fun, this application makes it easy to do so using natural hand movements.

How to Use the Application

Using the application is simple. Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Open the application on your computer or device.
  2. Allow the application to access your camera.
  3. Make a hand gesture in front of the camera.
  4. The application will recognize the gesture and take the corresponding action.

Supported Gestures

The following gestures are currently supported by the application:

  • Wave: Wave your hand to play or pause your media player.
  • Thumbs up: Give a thumbs up to increase the volume.
  • Thumbs down: Give a thumbs down to decrease the volume.
  • Peace sign: Make a peace sign to skip to the next track.
  • Fist: Make a fist to go back to the previous track.

Troubleshooting

If you are having trouble using the application, try the following:

  • Make sure that your camera is properly configured and working.
  • Ensure that you are making the gestures clearly and distinctly.
  • If the application is not responding to your gestures, try adjusting the lighting or distance between you and the camera.
